Design Notes

Full skeuomorphic turntable. Dark walnut cabinet with wood grain texture, corner screws, and warm overhead lighting. The record sits in a recessed platter well with a rubber mat texture. Machined aluminum transport buttons with bevel edges, press animation, and engraved labels. Green phosphor LCD panel (Casio/VFD-inspired) with pixel grid texture, scrolling track name, and segmented progress bar. Volume knob with knurled edge. Tonearm with counterweight, headshell, and arm rest. Brand plate reads "Nikolaus KD-1500 Digital." Speed selector toggles 33/45 RPM.
