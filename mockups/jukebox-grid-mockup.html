Sonos-style: Clean Tiles with Labels

Album art dominates each tile with 1-2 lines of text below for playlist name and type. Clean white space between tiles reduces cognitive load when scanning. Consistent square shapes create visual harmony. This is the closest match to Sonos's actual library browsing experience.

Clean Art + Text Scannable Touch-friendly

Tap album art to flip to Jukebox. Tap a playlist to simulate selection. Click "← Now Playing" to flip back.
